Eileen Flynn Bio

Eileen Flynn has had a solid career in developing people and organizations, globally, within three large, reputable companies – J&J, Honeywell, and Digital Equipment Corporation (DEC). She is now taking her wealth of knowledge and offering practical solutions to individuals, teams and organizations as part of Eileen Flynn Consulting, LLC. Eileen’s extensive career has allowed her to be a “jack of all trades” in the field of organization development. This helps her to give a holistic recommendation to her clients. She is known as an expert, however, in the areas of business transformation, employee engagement, team development and coaching. She has worked around the globe and has a unique skill in cultural awareness.

Eileen has a core belief that people work best when they feel valued and their strengths are utilized. Her focus is on “bringing out the best in people” and the power of this approach simply multiplies as the audience increases. Magic happens as people flourish and deliver extraordinary results. What is the secret ingredient? It is Eileen’s passion, energy and experience. She rolls up her sleeves and puts her skin in the game. She gets to the heart of the issue by asking the right questions and making an accurate diagnosis. This leads to practical solutions that make sense.

Eileen instills confidence in her clients by her portfolio of successful applications such as: leading change, communications and training in an SAP implementation impacting 1200 end users at J&J, developing over 100 process improvement teams leading to 100% on time delivery at Honeywell, and turning around morale at DEC after massive layoffs. It’s never about theory; it’s about real, tried and true methods. Eileen simply customizes to whatever environment she is supporting which allows her to accelerate results for clients.

Feedback from clients about Eileen is consistent. She genuinely cares about us. She understands the connection between people and results and makes things happen. Her energy is contagious. She doesn’t just tell us what to do; she gets in there and involves us so that we learn and grow. Eileen is invested in our success. We know she is working with many clients, but when she is with us, we get her undivided attention as if we were her single focus.

Eileen has a Master of Arts in Counseling from Rider University and Bachelor of Science in Organizational Behavior from LaSalle University. She also spent a semester abroad at the University of Fribourg in Fribourg, Switzerland. She is certified in: Advanced Human Resource Management and Organization Development from Columbia University, Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI), Herrmann Brain Dominance Instrument (HBDI), DiSC. She has published two articles in the Organization Development Journal, Best Global Practices in Internal OD: Employee Engagement: Boosting Productivity in Turbulent Times, and What Makes Good Teams Work Better: Research-Based Strategies That Distinguish Top-Performing Cross-Functional Drug Development Teams.

On a personal note, Eileen has been married to Bob Schreck for over 25 years and together they raised their two children. Bryan and Nell have both graduated college and are working full time out on their own. Eileen enjoys her free time at the Jersey shore biking and walking along the beach. This is where she gets her energy and clarity that she brings to her clients.
