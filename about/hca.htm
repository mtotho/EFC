Human Capital Alliance

Eileen Flynn Consulting, LLC, is a member of the Human Capital Alliance. The HCA is a select group of experienced consultants who have come together to deepen and expand their capabilities and services to clients. Most members have worked together for over 15 years and have demonstrated that they can collaborate effectively to deliver extraordinary business results. The HCA members share the same values and trust one another to be accountable to each other’s client success. The strength of the alliance ensures that all human capital needs are met.

HCA Value Proposition

By leveraging our collective knowledge and expertise we are able to:

  • Provide superior quality services
  • Competitively price our solutions
  • Offer solutions that are practical to implement
  • Partner with you and your team for long-term success
  • Transfer our knowledge to build your capability

Expanded Offerings

Here is a summary of all the services the HCA collectively provides to our clients:

  • Executive Coaching - career transitions, enhancing current performance, preparing for future opportunities and assimilating into new cultures
  • Leadership Development – strategy development, competency modeling, action learning and skill focused learning experiences, competency resource development guides
  • Talent Management – strategy development, competency-based selection interviewing, performance management, succession management
  • High Performance Team Development - coaching and facilitation to all types of teams in areas such as team start up, new leader assimilation, team assessments, and developing high performing teams
  • Business Transformation and Change Management – SAP and other large system implementations, mergers and acquisitions, restructurings and downsizings, employee engagement
  • Project Management – business plan development, communication, execution and follow-up
  • Meeting Design and Facilitation – design, facilitation and follow-up of meetings to be both effective and efficient in achieving business objectives
  • Skill Focused Training – instructor led workshops to build capability in a variety of areas such as: influencing, decision making, facilitation, crucial conversations, conflict management and negotiations
  • Organizational Design - strategy, culture, work processes, structure and systems and staffing to configure an organization to foster achievement of valued business, customer and employee outcomes
  • Organizational and Process Improvement – assessing and analyzing organization processes, including work scope, activity flow, inputs and outputs, resources and controls for increased effectiveness and efficiency
  • Employee Engagement – coaching leaders on “how to” build employee commitment especially during large business transformations in areas such as rewards and recognition, career development, employee communications, retention programs and one-on-one employee coaching
