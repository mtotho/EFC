Service Offerings

Eileen Flynn Consulting, LLC has selected four specific focus areas where clients can expect a portfolio of successful applications.

  • Business Transformation
  • Employee Engagement
  • Team Development
  • Coaching

We will use the following approach with all our service offerings:

  1. Data Gathering and Relationship Building
    It is important to get to know you and learn about what is keeping you up at night. We will ask for just enough information to help us with an accurate diagnosis so that we can focus on the right solution. We will then come to an agreement with you on what is in and out of scope. A critical success factor in our work is knowledge transfer. We will ask to partner with people inside your company so that our deliverables are sustainable when the consulting engagement ends.
  2. Planning
    Once we are aligned on the scope of work we will develop a plan of action. This is typically a formal project plan that outlines specific deliverables and timeframes. We will work with you on an appropriate method of measuring success in this planning phase.
  3. Execution
    This is the heart of our work where the deliverables are met. A strong skill we bring to each of our engagements is meeting design and facilitation. All of our service offerings require us to bring people together to solve business issues. We do this in an efficient manner and have techniques for dealing with remote participants. We utilize our expertise and tools to accelerate our delivery; however, we are careful to customize to fit your company’s specific needs and culture.
  4. Follow-Up
    We don’t consider our work complete until your expectations of us are met. Depending on the topic, we will gain your buy-in to conduct interviews, focus groups or surveys to check progress. We will then discuss closure with you.

Note: We can assist you in finding support for other topics not listed above via our connection with the Human Capital Alliance (HCA).
